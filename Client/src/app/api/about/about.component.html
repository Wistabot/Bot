WiSTA

WiSTA (Wipro Software Technology Academy) is an 8-semester (4 years) off-campus collaborative M.Tech Programme with VIT offering the best IT platform, soft skills and live project exposure.

The WiSTA-VIT Partnership

The VIT University-WiSTA collaboration offering quality education to the talent pipeline of Wipro has completed more than a decade of operations and is testimonial to the Wipro commitment of nurturing and developing talent.
Vellore Institute of Technology (Vellore, Tamil Nadu) is a premier engineering institution in India. A globally recognized institution, the VIT has been conferred the 'Deemed University Status' in recognition of its academic excellence by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, and the UGC, New Delhi, apart from various international accreditations for its programs.
The pedagogy and evaluation methodologies were chosen and implemented by VIT in consultation with Wipro's Talent Transformation division while VIT arranges the faculty and awards the degree.

  • VIT University is accredited by NAAC VIT has international accreditations by ABET (USA), IET
  • (UK) Energy Institute (UK) for engineering Programs

Course Structure

The 48-month course is divided into eight semesters, with four courses per semester. The first semester will be the PRP program of Wipro. Contact classes for 16 weeks are conducted by VIT Faculty with four sessions over the weekend. During the week, you will be involved in working on live projects among various business domains in Wipro giving you first-hand knowledge and training.
The details of the WiSTA program offered by VIT are outlined below:
